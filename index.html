Tile Wizard - Ultimate Tiled Image Viewer

Tile Wizard helps you visualize tiled background images. It's ideal for viewing tiled outputs from popular image generation tools like Midjourney and Stable Diffusion. Simply upload your tiled image and click 'set background' to preview it seamlessly and in style.

To create a tiled image in Midjourney v5, pass the --tile parameter in your prompt: `/imagine field of clouds --tile`
